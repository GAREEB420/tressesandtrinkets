About Tresses & Trinkets

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by three best friends who shared a passion for beautiful hair accessories, Tresses & Trinkets began as a small dream in a college dorm room. Frustrated by the lack of quality scrunchies that were both stylish and gentle on hair, we decided to create our own.

What started as making scrunchies for ourselves and friends quickly grew into a beloved brand trusted by thousands of customers worldwide. We believe that every woman deserves to feel beautiful and confident, and our premium scrunchies are designed to make that happen.

Today, we're proud to offer a curated collection of the finest scrunchies made from premium materials like silk, satin, and organic cotton. Every piece is crafted with love and attention to detail, ensuring that you get both style and hair care in one beautiful accessory.
