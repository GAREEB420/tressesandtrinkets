Products

Our scrunchies are made from premium materials including 100% mulberry silk, organic cotton, satin, and velvet. Each material is carefully selected for its hair-friendly properties and durability.

We prioritize materials that are gentle on hair and help prevent breakage and creasing.

Yes! Our scrunchies are designed to work with all hair types, from fine to thick, straight to curly. The gentle materials and construction make them especially beneficial for those with delicate or chemically-treated hair.

Silk scrunchies are made from natural silk fibers and offer the ultimate in hair care benefits. They're naturally hypoallergenic, temperature-regulating, and extremely gentle on hair.

Satin scrunchies provide similar smooth texture benefits at a more affordable price point. While not natural like silk, they still offer excellent hair protection against friction and breakage.

Care Instructions

Silk scrunchies should be hand-washed or machine-washed on a gentle cycle with cold water and mild detergent. Always air dry - never put silk in the dryer.

For best results, place in a mesh laundry bag when machine washing and use silk-specific detergent when available.

Most of our scrunchies are machine washable on a gentle cycle. We recommend using a mesh laundry bag to protect them during washing.

Always check the care label on your specific scrunchie for detailed washing instructions.

We recommend washing your scrunchies every 1-2 weeks with regular use, or more frequently if you use heavy hair products.

Clean scrunchies not only look better but also help maintain healthy hair and scalp.
