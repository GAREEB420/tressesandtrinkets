Care Instructions

Washing:

  • Machine wash on gentle cycle with cold water
  • Use mild detergent (silk-specific detergent recommended)
  • Wash with similar colors
  • Place in mesh laundry bag for extra protection

Drying:

  • Air dry only - do not use machine dryer
  • Lay flat or hang to dry away from direct sunlight
  • Avoid wringing or twisting

Storage:

  • Store in a cool, dry place
  • Avoid storing in plastic bags for extended periods
  • Keep away from sharp objects that might snag the silk
